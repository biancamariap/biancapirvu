About me

Hello there, my name is Bianca Pîrvu and I am 21 years old. I am a Media Production student at FSPAC, Cluj-Napoca, Romania. I have a background in computer science and I love art, photography, traveling and fantasy books. Drawing and photography have always been a big part of my life. I enjoy creating and sharing my passion with the world.
